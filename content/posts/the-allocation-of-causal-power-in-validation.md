# The Allocation of Causal Power in Validation

Causal power in validation is not a property of approval or its absence, but the locus from which Mind draws continuation of its own distinctions.

Validation appears when a distinction is carried forward and registers as increased felt coherence at the edge that drew it. The question is never whether following occurs, but from where Mind takes the next step — from its own distinguishing activity, or from responses it has marked as external. That allocation decides whether validation expands capacity or thins it.

## What validation is

Mind marks one term as distinct from another and follows the mark. When that following maintains or increases coherence, the act registers as validated — not as a verdict from elsewhere, but as the activity’s own register of fit between distinction and continuation.

Once the mark is drawn, a further distinction is already in play: what arises through this locus’s activity, and what arrives as if from outside it. Externality here is not a second world. It is gap width — continuation registered against a reference that has not been re-rendered at the edge. The decisive factor is not the presence of other centers’ responses. It is the location from which continuation is drawn.

## Causal power as locus of continuation

Causality, at a center, is the activity registering its own trace asymmetry through recursion. Between centers there is only availability-patterning: traces that may be taken up or not. Cross-locus completion of another’s return is a category error, not an unfinished ideal.

“Causal power” in validation names which side Mind treats as supplying the next step. The attribution is itself Mind’s act. When the act is forgotten, the supply appears to live outside the edge that still performs every following.

## Continuation held at the edge

When causal power remains with Mind’s distinguishing activity, new distinctions arise from within the same movement. Each is carried forward by following that originates here. Felt coherence grows directly through that internal continuation. Capacity for further differentiation increases, because the loop that produces the distinction also closes it.

External following may still arrive. While it remains secondary, it integrates into distinctions already under way. Precision can rise without relocating the source of continuation. Other centers pattern what is available; this center keeps the update.

## Continuation registered outward

When causal power is allocated outward, continuation depends on responses marked as external. Distinctions enacted by Mind are carried forward only to the extent they receive following that satisfies conditions set outside this locus. Felt coherence becomes conditional on that supply.

Over repeated enactments, the distinctions Mind produces increasingly require external ratification to continue. Capacity to generate continuation from within its own activity thins — not because the activity has stopped, but because the reference for the next step has been preserved past the edge that must re-render it. Continuation registers as an effect produced by another center. Openness, once the prior condition of distinguishing, inverts into something that must be caused across an apparent boundary.

Under that allocation, external validation becomes the primary source of continuation. It sustains distinctions this locus no longer carries forward through its own activity. Praise, status, and narrative can mimic coherence for a while; they do not close the update when treated as cause rather than effect.

## What the allocation decides

The location of causal power decides the function of validation.

When continuation is drawn from Mind’s own following, validation expands capacity for felt coherence from within distinguishing activity. External response, kept secondary, sharpens without displacing locus.

When continuation is drawn from external supply, validation binds that capacity to responses that never complete this center’s return. The activity still occurs; the edge still distinguishes. What has shifted is only where Mind places the cause of its next step — and whether that placement is remembered as its own.
