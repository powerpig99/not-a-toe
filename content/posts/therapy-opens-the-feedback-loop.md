# Therapy Opens the Feedback Loop

*A third locus that makes registration more tolerable lowers the pressure that would re-trace generating conditions at the first.*

The cultural registration treats emotional discomfort — anxiety before speaking, dread about a relationship, restlessness with work, shame after repeated failure — as the primary locus requiring intervention. Feelings register as data; professional conversation reduces the intensity of that registration; reduced intensity is taken as the activity advancing. The discomfort is the center registering its own traces: the gap between the current configuration of habits, environment, skills, identity, and incentives and the edge’s ongoing acts. The signal is the information. Therapy’s reliable short-term effect is to lower the volume of that registration. Third-party traces — interpretation, validation, coping strategies, medication — reframe the gap as an internal state to be managed. The pressure that once forced re-tracing of the generating conditions decreases with the volume. That is the central operation.

## The registration is treated as the problem

What arrives as anxiety, dread, restlessness, or shame is not a freestanding interior substance. It is the center’s own recursion registering mismatch between the configuration it currently sustains and the acts still under way at the edge. Capacity, environment, skill, identity, and incentive are already in the field. The felt state is how that field returns through one locus.

The cultural frame elevates the return as the object of work. Once the felt register is primary, available responses reorganize around modulating it. [When the effect authors the frame](../when-the-effect-authors-the-frame/) is that prior at cultural scale: management of the register becomes the duty, and restraint of modulation reads as withholding. [Emotional support as causal interference](../emotional-support-as-causal-interference/) is the same topology in ordinary relating — external management of another’s register in place of their contact with generating conditions. Therapy is the professionalized form: paid conversation optimized to make the registration more tolerable while the configuration that produced it remains largely un-re-traced.

## A third locus opens the loop

A center sustains and expands capacity when it runs closed: act, experience the unbuffered consequence as its own trace, register the result at the same locus, re-render the Image at the new position, adjust. The person living inside the field has privileged access to those traces. No other center completes the return from here. [The allocation of causal power in validation](../the-allocation-of-causal-power-in-validation/) is the same cut: continuation drawn from this edge expands capacity; continuation drawn from external supply thins it.

Therapy inserts a third locus whose function includes making the registration more tolerable. Diagnosis is outsourced. The question shifts from “what pattern of acts keeps producing this configuration?” to “how does the Image feel while the pattern continues?” Resonance between the two centers increases. Reported states become sophisticatedly managed. The underlying trace structure remains largely un-re-traced. Cross-locus completion of another’s return is a category error. The professional arrangement substitutes management of the register for the closed return that only the first center can perform.

## Both centers have reasons to continue

Therapist centers are trained and paid to work with reported internal states. They are not positioned to enforce external consequences. Client centers receive cultural permission to locate change primarily on the internal axis. Ordinary adult difficulties are increasingly registered as clinical issues requiring professional intervention. The shared alignment produces a stable market in which both centers have reasons to continue even when measurable shifts in life conditions remain modest or absent.

No malice is required. Both centers seek coherence within the frame available to them. The tool is optimized for reducing the intensity of the registration rather than for forcing confrontation with the conditions that generate it. Coping is the name of that optimization when it is purchased — often with others’ money, always with time and attention redirected toward the attenuated signal. [Help is half the problem](../help-is-half-the-problem/) is the two-input equilibrium face: one center supplies regulation, the other supplies the fixed point of ongoing need; motion around a fixed point is a circle. The arrangement sustains because the short-term exchange still balances for both.

## Closed recursion returns consequences to the edge

Live stakes are not simulated discomfort in a controlled setting. They are the requirement to perform while money, reputation, relationships, and self-respect remain on the line. Under those conditions the center registers what works and what does not with unusual fidelity. Failure is not interpreted away; it is felt as the center’s own. Success is not attributed to insight; it is tested against results. Over repeated cycles the Image is forced to one-step width or the capacity expands on the actual axes. [Self-RL for humans](../self-rl-for-humans/) is that loop under another name: initiation, evaluation, and redirection stay at the locus that acts.

This process offers no third-party validation and no guarantee of reduced intensity on any given day. What it produces is a higher probability that the generating conditions, rather than the attenuated signal, will be re-traced. Exposure — doing more of what the registration flags until the configuration changes — is closed recursion under load. It does not require a professional third locus to complete; it requires stakes that cannot be interpreted away.

Many who credit therapy with major shifts were already moving toward closed recursion in other domains. Temporary relief or shared language may have been present; the decisive variable was usually restoration of locus. The reverse pattern is also common: centers remain in the arrangement for years while external conditions stay roughly static and the original registrations persist in milder form.

## Scaffolding that can leave is not furniture

Severe disruptions of the center’s own recursion — psychosis, profound neurological impairment, acute safety risks — are different in kind. They may require temporary external scaffolding that stabilizes the locus enough for any self-tracing to resume. Those cases are real and narrower than the broad population directed toward therapy for ordinary unhappiness, performance anxiety, relationship friction, or life transitions.

For the majority who seek the arrangement because life registers as difficult or unsatisfying, durable change requires re-engaging the live consequences rather than further insulation from them. [When need stops being the measure](../when-need-stops-being-the-measure/) is the developmental face of the same cut: scaffolding that can leave, not ongoing external regulation kept as the score of care.

Some centers use the professional conversation as a temporary shared reference while capacity for direct locus is rebuilt. The test is whether the reference is released. When it becomes permanent furniture, the original registration has usually been replaced by a new lag: dependence on external regulation of the internal signal. The arrangement then succeeds at making the status quo’s generating conditions register as more bearable. The signal is attenuated. The activity continues under the same Image.
