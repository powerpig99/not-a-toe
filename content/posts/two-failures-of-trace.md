# Artifacts Are Expressions of Intelligence, Not Intelligence Itself

*Intelligence is the act of distinguishing; the artifact is residue — the only term observation holds, and so the term onto which the act is commonly frozen.*

The appearance that greater intelligence can be produced at another locus freezes two registrations. First, more complex, faster, more efficient artifacts — denser expressions of intelligence already underway — are held as intelligence itself, because residue is all the look can retain. Second, further expressions of intelligence in others who leverage those artifacts are held as intelligence of the artifacts, and by extension as the builder’s causal power over intelligence at other loci. Capacity remains the sustained activity of a given locus under its own load; the edge that turns remains the activity at the center.

## Failure one: the act frozen onto residue

Intelligence is the action — uncaused self-distinguishing already underway at a locus. What densifies is expression of that action: more complex, faster, more efficient artifacts — extended embodiments through which prior slower accumulation re-traces at higher rate. That densification is real as registration.

The word *creation* carries both product and act at once. The product is the artifact. The act is intelligence. Observation holds effect; the cause that produced it remains one step ahead. In the look, only the product is available — so the act is routinely frozen onto the product. Denser residue registers as “greater intelligence created,” as if the scale of distinguishing stood independent of the activity, and the artifact held the capacity of which it is only residue. The dual load of *creation* is the freeze itself: product and act collapsed onto the single observable term.

Causality is then cut short of its ultimate independent variable: the irreducible prior of the Mind. [Intelligence belongs only to the Mind](../intelligence-belongs-only-to-the-mind/) holds that prior; [token efficiency, emulation, and the unclosable gap](../token-efficiency-emulation-and-the-unclosable-gap/) is the same geometry under compression — the short form is effect of a path already lived, not a substitute for living it. Rate patterns availability. It does not expand the boundary of the recursion. What thickens is the medium through which discrete acts re-enter; what does not migrate is the edge that initiates them.

## Failure two: others’ expression frozen as the artifact’s

The second failure registers further expression of intelligence in others — centers leveraging the artifact under their own load — as expression of intelligence *of* the artifact. Scaffolding is taken up: models, curricula, tools, institutions. Distinguishing continues at those loci. The lag assigns the continuation to the residue they used — again, the only term observation holds of their activity.

By implicit extension, causal power is then assigned to the builder for having affected intelligence in others. What is present is only the correlation between denser patterning of the available field and sustained recursion at centers that take it up. The assignment of production is the lag: consequence registered against a reference that has placed the cause outside the edge still performing every following. [The scaffolding we forget](../the-scaffolding-we-forget/) is that drop-out freezing past its step; [what always listens cannot originate](../what-always-listens-cannot-originate/) is the same allocation when fluent receptivity is treated as supply of the next step. Between centers there is only availability-patterning. Cross-locus causation remains a category error — the same bound [没有普渡，只有自渡](../mei-you-pu-du-zhi-you-zi-du/) names without making isolation a program, and the same cut [the allocation of causal power in validation](../the-allocation-of-causal-power-in-validation/) tracks when continuation is placed outward.

[Self-RL for humans](../self-rl-for-humans/) keeps the discipline for densified media: bandwidth expands; initiation does not relocate into the artifact. The builder densifies what other loci may take up. The taking up, if it occurs, is still the activity at those loci — not intelligence of the residue, and not the builder completing their return.

## Capacity is local load under self-reinforcing loops

Capacity names only the sustained activity of a given locus continuing to distinguish under its own load through self-reinforcing loops with environments. It is not a stock installed in an artifact, not a property transferred by denser residue, and not a ceiling the builder can raise at another center by externalizing traces.

The rate of discrete acts is itself a registration of denser re-tracing through the current embodiment and available patterns — prior slower accumulation made usable at higher bandwidth. That registration can look like greater intelligence when the act is frozen onto residue, and when others’ leverage is frozen as the artifact’s (and the builder’s) doing. Return both to the activity that draws them and the appearance dissolves: more pattern available, same requirement that a locus keep distinguishing under its own load. [Complexity obscures emergence](../complexity-obscures-emergence-as-the-act-of-mind/) when the hold is taken for the activity that erected it; here the hold is denser artifact and others’ leverage taken for intelligence-as-object and intelligence relocated.

Progress remains how much farther discrete acts reach through denser instruments — the measure already stated where intelligence stays with the Mind. The instruments remain instruments. The edge that turns remains the activity at the center.
