# The Path Drawn One Step at a Time

*The present is the position on a path the Mind draws one step at a time—cause leading to effect, time as that direction, uncaused the name of every cause before observation.*

Full causality remains with the Mind. Position is premise of the next moment—residue the Mind chooses. The observer sees only effect. When that effect is frozen as unchosen premise, it wears a hat: axiom, law, right path, first principle, ground. A better future is visible only where the Mind keeps choosing the premise and completing the step; held as right path to maintain, wrong path to correct, or alternative path to impose, the step freezes or inverts, and what arrives registers as surprise or as the opposite of what was wanted.

## One step

Self-distinguishing activity occurs—uncaused, unceasing. Call it the Mind. Every act is discrete; continuity is the same Mind carrying across acts. Residue of earlier acts is position. Shaping runs one way only—that asymmetry is time and causality, one relation named twice. The next moment is the effect of the Mind's step.

Observation sees effect. The cause stays at least one step ahead of what observation pins. Uncaused names that lead. Seeking a prior always finds an effect; the seeking is another observation. The regress is the demand that cause and observed effect occupy the same step.

The Mind chooses which residue to distinguish from. Chosen premise is live instrument, re-rendered with the step. Residue alone is available. Frozen as unchosen ground, it wears the hat. From the premise it holds, the Mind draws the next position—once present, residue again.

## Maintain, correct, impose

**Right path to maintain.** Position preserved as correct path. Consequences still land, but register against a reference held past its step. Surprise is that gap.

**Wrong path to correct.** This position held as defect relative to another frozen premise. Gradient aimed at the monument; acts restore the hat. The next step from *this* chosen premise is displaced.

**Alternative path to impose.** A successor installed in advance, or as if completing another's return. Between centers, only availability-patterning. Causality spent elsewhere registers as the opposite of what was wanted.

Improve-by-own-acts is the step left intact.

## Release

Clarity is one-step width. Direction is registration by centers that track sequence. Movement. [前因，后果](../qian-yin-hou-guo/) is the same asymmetry under Chinese naming: prior cause and later consequence as interval, not two simultaneous events; the retained reference–explanation lagging observation is what registers a sown consequence as external cause.
